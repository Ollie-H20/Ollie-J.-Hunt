About me!

My name is Ollie. I am a software developer currently living between Leeds and Shropshire. Despite previously attaining a degree in Biomedical Science, I have found a love for web development. As part of the #100DEVS agency, I took part in their training bootcamp which gave me the skills to successfully build a career within Software Engineering.

I have previous project experience in the field, and I am really excited to expand my portfolio. I am keen to build on my knowledge, helping small local businesses extend their reach online whilst also developing my own skills and knowledge.

I pride myself on my great communication skills, something I believe is of utmost importance when delivering the needs and wants of the client. Rather than providing a service lacking in the desires of the customer, I want to be able to incorporate my web development knowledge with the customers own ideas!

Let's work together...

My aim is to provide 100% customer satisfaction. An initial consultation will be carried out in order to ascertain your desires and needs from your website. Also getting an idea of how you want your site to look and feel will be an important first conversation. Once the first draft is complete, we can work together to streamline the site to your desire.

If you have any questions regarding my services, then please be in touch using my contact info at the bottom of the page!
